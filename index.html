Hi, I'm a developer that loves clean & elegant code.

A little bit about me.

I'm based in Kailua, Hawaii with my family. My journey into tech started because I was a stay at home mom that wanted to work and also continue to have the flexibility of being present for my family. I wanted to learn HTML and CSS to fit my own specific career goals, so I started taking online courses and putting what I learned into practice.

After years of trying to find my place in the workforce as we moved every couple years for my husband's career, I realized that web development could give me the freedom & versatility I was looking for while also satisfying my creative spirit.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well–formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble–shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is a pixel–perfect match.
